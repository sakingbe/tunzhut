Babo Sammo

Akingbe Ayodeji Apple Music Spotify

​Babo Sammo is a Nigerian professional known for his expertise in computer and phone technology, as well as his contributions to music. With approximately six years of experience in the field, he specializes in both software and hardware solutions. Babo is the founder of Safer Naija, a platform dedicated to helping individuals recover stolen or misplaced items. ​ In addition to his technical pursuits, Babo Sammo has ventured into music, releasing singles such as "Tribute to my Guardian" and "Fake Ajebutters" in 2021. His diverse skill set also includes website design and development, showcasing his multifaceted talents.
